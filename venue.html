Venue

The ceremony and celebration will be held in a sodyba “Vila RA”. It is a beautiful Villa, featuring traditional Lithuanian attributes and style. The venue is surrounded by nature and faces the lake. As the typical Lithuanian wedding, the venue is both elegant and down to nature — exactly how we like it!

As we fell in-love to the landscape and the lake, we have also decided to make a ceremony right there, on the shore of the lake, where we can get inspired by the true spirits of the Mother Earth. It means that you won’t have to change places after the ceremony and can enjoy a full day celebration without a need of travelling.

Besides the beautiful environment for the ceremony and a hall for the reception, the Villa also has accommodation so that the party would continue up to the dawn! More about sleeping and other venue-related peculiarities read in ``Practical information’’.

How to get there?

  • The address of the place: Ateities g. 2, Inturkė 33211, Lithuania.
  • Coordinates: 55.161279, 25.5471242.

Driving directions

Vila Ra is ~70km from Vilnius. Taking the “Molėtai road” drive up to Molėtai crossing and turn right (Molėtai is on the left side). Drive around 12km up to reaching Inturkė.

From there, you should enter into the first asphalt road on the right (where the sign “wedding” is leading).

Follow the main road till you see the bright blue mosque and turn right again. You are almost there! Follow the road, always keeping right (two more turns) and you’ll face the entrance with the P (parking) sign directing you to the left.

Park your car and you’re welcome!

For those that has no possibilities to arrive there by their own transport or prefer to leave your car at home, rental transport will be organised. We would ask you to inform us about your preferences together with RSVP.
