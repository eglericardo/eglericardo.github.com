There's more!

This wedding is going to be the celebration of the union between two hearts that lived most of their life thousands of kilometers apart and found each other in the midway (in Italy). Therefore, it will be the extraordinary, international wedding, uniting not only two people but also two cultures—Lithuanian and Brazilian. We hope that during the day you will be able to discover a little bit of both cultures, being it through food, games or dances.

Although the wedding will be held in Lithuania, we encourage you to embrace the international environment and engage into the journey of discoveries with an open-mind. As the two, very distinctive cultures will meet, there is no doubt that some challenges to communicate will appear, but we hope that good mood and skills in hand language will break the barrier and make it a day to remember!
