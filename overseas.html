Staying in Vilnius

As a very beautiful city and the geographical center of Europe, Vilnius is used with and prepared for flows of curious tourists, entrepreneurs and travelers that enjoy their time in this Baltic jewel—according to tourism guides, of course: we are modest!

Therefore, Vilnius has a great choice of hotels (more expensive) and bed-and-breakfasts (beautiful, private and good price) where you can rest yourself after a day of walk through our beautiful “prospektai” (avenues) or jogging in our woods.
